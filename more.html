What Is Gudie ?

Gudie is for every student studying any computer-related field — whether it's Computer Engineering, Computer Science, AI, or others — wherever you are in the world.


It was created to support your unique learning journey by lovingly gathering the best educational resources for every computer-related subject.


Gudie is the first step in a journey of a thousand miles, built by a student like you — a beginner project with room to grow and improve.


If you have any feedback, suggestions, or criticism, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Your opinion truly matters
